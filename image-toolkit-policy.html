1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Image Toolkit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and password.
  • User Content: Any content you create, upload, or share through the app (e.g., photos, comments).

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use the app, such as features used, time spent, and interactions.
  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we may collect precise or approximate location information from your mobile device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Communicate with you, including sending updates and support messages.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our app.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who perform services on our behalf.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.

5. Your Choices

  • Location Information: You can disable location services for our app through your device settings.
  • Push Notifications: You can opt out of receiving push notifications through your device settings.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@imagetoolkit.com.
